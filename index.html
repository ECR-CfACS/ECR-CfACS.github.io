The 2nd CfACS Annual ECR and PhD Conference

On Friday the 24th of June 2022, we welcome our ECRs and PhD students to present their work and take part in networking opportunities with colleagues across the University.

Sign Up »

Overview

The CfACS ECR and PhD conference provides a forum for ERs and PhD students across the CfACS research centre to present their research, network and gain collaboration opportunities within CfACS and the University as a whole. The conference is part of the continuous effort to better support the ECR community within CfACS and the Department of Computing and Mathematics, encouraging and structuring those at the early stages of their research career. The event will consist of five themes, aligning with the research themes of the research centre. More information on external speakers and the overall agenda will follow shortly. This Conference has been led and organised by Dr John Henry and Dr Connah Kendrick with extensive support from Professor Yonghong Peng and Professor Darren Dancey. All attendees and presenters/speakers must sign up to help cater for people accordingly.
